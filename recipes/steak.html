Steak

Pan seared steak, perfect for a date!

    Ingredients:
  • 1-1.5 lb boneless ribeye
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 4 sprigs thyme
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

    • Tools:
  • 10 inch cast iron pan
  • Baking tray with wire rack
  • Meat thermometer
    Steps:
  1. Pat the steak with paper towels until dry
  2. Salt and pepper all sides and put on wire rack in fridge overnight
  3. Heat the oil in a pan on high heat until heavy smoking
  4. Put steak in and flip every 30 seconds. Lower heat if smoke is excessive
  5. When steak reaches around 85-90 degrees add butter, thyme, and smashed garlic clove
  6. Baste the steak and continue to flip until temp reaches 120 fahrenheit
  7. Let it rest for 5 minutes. After that it is done
