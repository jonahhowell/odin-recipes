Chicken

Pan fried chicken. Tasty and healthy

    Ingredients
  • Boneless chicken breast w/o tenderloin
  • 1.5 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

    • Tools
  • 10 inch stainless steel pan
  • Western chef knife
  • Plastic wrap
  • Meat pounder, or something big, flat, and heavy
  • Tinfoil
  • Meat thermometer
    Steps
  1. Cut chicken breast in half
  2. Wrap both halves in plastic and pound to 1/4 inch thickness
  3. Lightly flour one side of each breast
  4. Heat oil in pan until lightly smoking
  5. Lay chicken in floured side down, and let cook for 2.5 minutes
  6. Flip and take out when internal temp reaches 155 degrees fahrenheit
  7. Let rest for 5 minutes under tinfoil before eating
